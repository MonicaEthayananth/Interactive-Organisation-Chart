Colleagues,


The UT Share Hardware Infrastructure Migration Project will be going live this weekend.


What is happening: UT Share Finance and Human Resources applications will be offline in support of upgrades to the network and servers. We do not anticipate any impacts to MyMav.


A link will be provided to the upgraded system once the process has been completed.


When is This Happening: Starting at 11 p.m today, Friday, April 7, through 8 a.m. Tuesday, April 11.


Who is Affected: All users of UT Share

Who Do I Contact for Questions or Concerns: Please contact the Business Support Desk (utsharehelp@uta.edu) via e-mail with any issues
