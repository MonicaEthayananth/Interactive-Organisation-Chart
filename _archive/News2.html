October 13, 2016

UTShare WorkFlow Migration Timeline

What is Happening?

Workflow is being integrated into UTShare on Monday, November 7, 2016. This means that approvals for Finance Documents will no longer be processed in UTAFlow except Budget Transfers.

Approvals for Vouchers, Requisitions, Expense Reports, Travel Authorizations, Cash Advances and Journal Entries will be done internally in UTShare.

What Actions are being taken to prepare for this?

On Thursday, November 3, 2016 at noon, the ability to create vouchers will be temporarily suspended until opening of business Monday, November 7, 2016.

On Thursday, November 3, 2016 at noon, creation of all Finance Documents except Budget Transfers in UTAFlow will be disabled. Approvals for pre-existing UTAFlow documents will be allowed to be completed by using the link in the automated email.

Why is this being done?

Vouchers created before WorkFlow are the only documents created in an already “Approved” state in UTShare and will go straight to a paid status. In order to prevent unapproved payments, we are removing the ability to create vouchers from campus. This will allow Accounts Payable ample time to review and process those Pre-Workflow Vouchers without error.

If you have questions, please do not hesitate to contact the Manager of Disbursements, Randall Piatkowski, ext. 2-0404.
